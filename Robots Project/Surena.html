Surena IV

4th Gen Iranian Humanoid Robot

Surena IV is the latest fruit of 11 years of hard work by engineers at the Center for Advanced Systems and Technology at the University of Tehran. The aim of the project is to build a practical robot that will be able to replace humans in many dangerous activities.

Watching the latest video presenting the interesting capabilities of the robot, we may be tempted to say that research experiments are progressing in a very good direction. Although the efficiency of the Surena IV cannot be compared to, for example, the famous ATLAS robot from Boston Dynamics, it is still a great achievement for the country where it is built..

Surena IV was unveiled in 2019 but the video recording that just came out. While Surena IV cannot do parkour like the Atlas robot from Boston Dynamics, it comes with other more subtle developments that are worth the attention. Its balance capability has been improved, it can now interact with more objects, and the robot is closer than ever to being able to do almost everything a typical human can do
